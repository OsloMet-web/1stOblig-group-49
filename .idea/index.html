The internet today is the most reliable form of world wide web. This phenomenon has today become as big as millions of people being reliant of the internet its infrastructure to be able to fulfil its desires. Whether that is to be able to work or visit family relatives. Such as any innovation of this gratitude, this has caused major political responsibilities. Internet today has the largest infrastructure of it all. With this comes the political implications that follows. This means that internet influence the distribution of power and resources between groups. And example of internet being used as a political tool is in the Cambridge Analytica scandal. The university Cambridge Analytica is a British consulting firm that collected data from millions of Facebook users and used this data to provide analytical assistance to the 2016 American election of Ted Cruz and Donald Trump (Chang, 2018). This is to show of internets infrastructure being handled in an unethical way, in order to distribute power and resources.
